Current Network Situation — Visualisation Options

Three ways to show where peers stand right now relative to your node. Uses live peer data: height, status, cumulative difficulty.

A — Height Scatter

All peers plotted on a single height axis, relative to your tip. Clusters reveal consensus groups instantly. Simple and data-dense.

Most readable

B — Chain Lanes

Each distinct chain (grouped by cumulative difficulty) gets its own lane. Peer count + cumDiff bar shows which chain is winning. Great for multi-fork situations.

Most structural

C — Peer Constellation

Your node at center. Each peer orbits at a distance = how far their height deviates. Rings mark on-chain / stale / forking zones. Forking peers have visible spokes.

Most visual
